## Community pattern notes (paraphrased in our voice)
- The room costs about as much as the sensor; first-timers budget only the launch monitor and are blindsided by the screen/enclosure/projector/mat/PC total.
- "Build from the bottom up" — lock mat/screen/projector/netting first, then spend what's left on the sensor.
- Reserve an extra 10-15% of hardware cost for hidden/first-year items; the buffer rule is near-universal in 2026 guides.

## Credible warnings / caveats
- Radar units (R10, MLM2PRO) need depth behind the hitter; shallow rooms push you to photometric.
- Bushnell Launch Pro TCO climbs fast: Silver ~$199/yr, Gold ~$499/yr, plus ~$250/yr GSPro.
- Overhead units (EYE XO2, Falcon) demand 9-10 ft ceilings — architectural, not optional.
- Subscriptions are recurring forever; they belong in the budget, not as a footnote.
