## Community pattern notes (paraphrased, never quoted)

- **"You resell the base first."** Recurring r/simracing wisdom: the base is the most replaceable part; regret concentrates on the brand-locked rims and pedals bought around it, not the base itself. → used in the forum callout.
- **R9 vs R12 near-price-parity.** MOZA owners repeatedly note the R9 and R12 street prices sit within a few dollars, making the R9 an awkward pick unless deeply discounted. → used in the pro-tip callout.
- **Firmware-as-upgrade goodwill.** Fanatec's free torque bump was widely received as a rare pro-consumer move that quietly re-tiers existing hardware. → framed in Fanatec section without hype.

## Warnings surfaced in the article

1. Fanatec QR2 FFB lock-in on non-Fanatec rims (biggest gotcha).
2. Simagic is a hard console gate — no PS5/Xbox workaround.
3. Firmware changes the spec (ClubSport DD/DD+ torque) — check current firmware.
4. 12Nm+ needs a rigid 4-bolt mount, not a desk clamp (brand-agnostic).
5. Fanatec entry bases (CSL DD, GT DD Pro) need the right PSU/Boost Kit for full torque; bundles climb fast.
