## Community pattern notes (paraphrased in our voice)
- The repeating r/golfsimulator confession: "$3K launch monitor, $99 mat, now my left wrist clicks." Nobody regrets overspending on the mat.
- Owners of tall mats (esp. Softy at 2.5 in) discover ceiling/overhead-monitor clearance problems AFTER purchase — the quiet cause of many "my numbers are weird" threads.
- Replaceable strips (Softy, HotShot) are a repeated recommendation for high-volume practicers: wear one strip, not one mat.

## Credible warnings / caveats
- Mat height (Softy 2.5 in; Fiberbuilt/CCE 1.75 in) raises the ball above (a) a floor/overhead sensor's reference plane and (b) your feet on bare concrete — both distort club-path data. Measure FINISHED floor height, not the slab.
- Stand level with the ball: platform mat or matched landing turf, or the launch monitor reports a club path you did not swing.
- CCE is firmer to some players on fat strikes; joint-first buyers should lean Fiberbuilt or Softy.
