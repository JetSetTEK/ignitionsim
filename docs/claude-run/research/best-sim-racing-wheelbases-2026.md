## Community pattern notes (paraphrased in our voice)
- Repeated confession that owners of 18Nm+ bases run them at 60–70% and never revisit. Torque headroom is for heavy/no-assist cars, not lap time.
- "Buy pedals before more torque" is the most repeated regret — brake feel (load cell) matters more than base grip for most.
- Fanatec ecosystem lock-in (QR2 disabling FFB on non-Fanatec rims) is the recurring caution, above warranty worry post-Corsair.

## Credible warnings / caveats
- Big bases (12Nm+) on desk clamps flex, creak, and can damage desks — 4-bolt mount + rigid stand required.
- Entry bases (CSL DD, GT DD Pro) only reach higher torque with the optional Boost Kit 180.
- Firmware changes real torque numbers — always check current firmware.
