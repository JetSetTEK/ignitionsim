## Community pattern notes (paraphrased in our voice)
- The recurring r/golfsimulator confession: buy radar, jam it against the back wall, then blame the software. Radar in a shallow room isn't broken — it's extrapolating.
- "The subscription is the real price" — GSPro (~$250/yr) + gaming PC is the true first-year number, repeated across budget threads.
- Used-SkyTrak buyers regret skipping the account-transfer check more than any hardware flaw.

## Credible warnings / caveats
- Radar (R10, Mevo) needs depth behind the hitter; shallow rooms degrade accuracy — the #1 budget regret.
- "Under $1,000" is a moving target: SkyTrak, Bushnell, FlightScope Mevo all crossed the line in 2026. Old reviews mislead.
- Photometric units (Square, SkyTrak) want consistent indoor lighting.
- MLM2PRO measured spin requires RPT-marked balls; otherwise spin is estimated.
- Subscription stack: some units carry two fees (device Premium + GSPro).
