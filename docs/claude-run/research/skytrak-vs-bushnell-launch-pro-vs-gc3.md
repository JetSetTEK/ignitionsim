## Community pattern notes (paraphrased in our voice)
- The most-repeated regret in this tier is buying the sensor and forgetting the software line item — Launch Pro owners who priced $2,499 and got surprised by Gold + GSPro at renewal.
- Subscription-averse buyers consistently land on SkyTrak or the straight GC3 specifically to avoid the January bill.
- GC3 vs GC3S confusion is real: several buyers grabbed the S for the lower sticker and only later understood the recurring fee.

## Credible warnings / caveats
- SkyTrak third-party connector availability for NEW customers is changing — do not assume old broad GSPro/E6/TGC compatibility on a fresh unit. Verify at retailer. (Body says "check current.")
- Launch Pro is half a product on ball-data-only; club data + sim require a tier or the $1,500 unlock.
- GC3S carries an annual fee and is being phased out; the no-subscription promise is straight-GC3-only.
- All three are photometric — indoor lighting consistency is mandatory.
