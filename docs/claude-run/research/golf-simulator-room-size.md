## Community pattern notes (paraphrased in our voice)
- The evergreen r/golfsimulator confession: "bought the launch monitor, measured the room after." Reframed as the article's spine — the room decides the hardware.
- Repeated caution that garage-door tracks / ducts / joists silently cut usable ceiling below the drywall number.

## Credible warnings / caveats
- Ceiling measured to lowest obstruction, not drywall.
- Radar 7–9 ft behind-ball depth is non-negotiable for accurate Doppler.
- Projector inside 8 ft risks ball-strike on lens; past 14 ft golfer casts shadow.
- 11 ft width is offset-hitting only; dual-handed wants 14–16 ft.
- Photometric camera units need controlled, even lighting (sun/flicker cause misreads).
