## Community / reviewer pattern notes (paraphrased in our voice)
- The most repeated budget-build regret: dumping money into torque (18Nm) and skimping on the brake. Balanced 12Nm + load cell out-laps unbalanced 18Nm + pot pedals.
- "Buy pedals before more torque" — a pressure-sensing load cell is the biggest consistency upgrade and carries across every future base over USB.
- G29/G920 vs DD debate is over at the $1,000 budget — DD wins outright; the belt bundles are only for a few-hundred-dollar budget.

## Credible warnings / caveats
- A 12Nm base flexes/creaks/damages desks — a rigid stand or cockpit is mandatory, not a someday upgrade. Desk clamp is fine only for 5Nm on a heavy desk.
- Bolt the pedals to the same rigid structure as the wheel (heel plate) or the load cell's repeatability is wasted.
- Fake "hydraulic" Amazon clone pedals train the wrong force curve — a real load cell beats a fake hydraulic.
- Prices swing $100+ on sale timing — verify before checkout; R12 and CRP2 both move.
