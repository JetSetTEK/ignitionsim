## Community pattern notes (paraphrased in our voice)

- The single most-upvoted correction on r/golfsimulator: buy the software before the sensor. People buy a launch monitor first, then discover their preferred title won't talk to it, or that it only runs on a Windows gaming PC they didn't budget for.
- Recurring regret: underestimating the monitor-specific membership stacked on top of the base software sub. Rapsodo Premium + GSPro, Uneekor Pro Package + GSPro, and Bushnell Gold + GSPro are all "two subs, not one."
- Frequent advice: if you can't decide on software, buy the most software-agnostic sensor (SkyTrak+ is the recurring example — GSPro + E6 + TGC 2019 + Awesome Golf) so no single compatibility list can trap you.

## Credible warnings / caveats

- **Windows-PC fork is the real gate.** GSPro, TGC 2019, and FSX Play are Windows-gaming-PC-only. iPad/Mac builders are limited to E6 Connect and Awesome Golf. This eliminates three of five platforms for no-PC buyers.
- **Monitor-specific subs stack on top of software subs.** GSPro on Foresight/Bushnell needs FSX 2020/Play (Gold on Launch Pro); on Uneekor needs Pro Package; on Rapsodo MLM2PRO needs Rapsodo Premium.
- **GC3 vs GC3S is a recurring-fee trap.** Straight GC3 = lifetime FSX Play, no fee. GC3S / Bushnell Launch Pro = ~$499/yr Gold.
- **TGC 2019 is aging.** Narrow, harder-to-set-up compatibility; Foresight not listed; newer budget monitors (R10, Launch Pro) inconsistent — verify the exact model.
- Prices and tiers change often — every price in the body is paired with "check current" language or a source-shelf link.
