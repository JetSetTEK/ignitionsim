## Community pattern notes (paraphrased in our voice)
- The loudest r/flightsim HOTAS complaint isn't hardware — it's MSFS 2024 quietly swapping you onto a default profile after updates so binds appear to vanish. Fix: map by hand, export the profile, never "Reset to Defaults."
- Warthog is the near-universal "buy it for life" pick, but the 2026 consensus adds a caveat: newer bases beat it on adjustable springs and modularity, and its ball-gimbal center detent hurts precision in non-A-10 aircraft.
- VKB Gladiator is the default value recommendation for anyone flying civil without a yoke.
- "A bare stick is half a HOTAS" — budget a throttle (and rudder) alongside any non-all-in-one stick.

## Credible warnings / caveats
- Warthog has no twist rudder — pedals are mandatory, not optional.
- Warthog street price crept above official MSRP (~$500 -> ~$580); always check current.
- VelocityOne Flightdeck is PC-only despite the "console" family branding — do NOT recommend it to Xbox flyers (that's the Flight Stick).
- Entry pot-based sticks (T.Flight) develop slop over time; contactless/Hall sticks don't.
- MSFS 2024 has no "Reset to Defaults" safety on the Controls page in the sane way other categories do — reset wipes everything.
