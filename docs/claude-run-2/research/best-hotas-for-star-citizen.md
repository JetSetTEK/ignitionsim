## Community pattern notes (paraphrased in our voice)
- "Buy the T.16000M first, decide later" is the near-universal entry recommendation; serious cargo/mining pilots outgrow the 30-button count within months.
- The throttle is the part to research per-SKU, not the stick: TWCS spring stiff out of box; some Orion 2 throttles ship with stiction; Virpil sells in waves and is going EOL on the CM3. The joystick you'll almost always love.
- The bind list outruns the buttons regardless of HOTAS — a labeled macro panel (Stream Deck) is the highest-impact add for a bind-heavy pilot.
- HOSAS wins pure combat (analog lateral/vertical strafe); HOTAS wins cargo/mining/cruise (physical speed detent). Many own both and swap by activity.

## Credible warnings / caveats
- Light baseplates slide under input (T.16000M especially, but metal bases creep too); a HOTAS needs two Monstertech mounts (stick + throttle).
- Config software (VKBDevCfg, SimAppPro, VPC) is a real evening of setup, not an afterthought; a mistuned curve feels worse than a cheaper stick tuned right.
- Prices/stock swing weekly on boutique throttles — every price is "around $X, check current."
- Virpil MongoosT-50CM3 is buy-it-while-you-can (final 2026 batch) — do not present as evergreen.
