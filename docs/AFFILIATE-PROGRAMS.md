# IgnitionSim Affiliate Program Directory

**Last researched:** 2026-06-14 · **Owner:** Robert Pruitt · **Site:** IgnitionSim.com

This is the monetization spine for IgnitionSim — a discipline-by-discipline map of which sim-hardware **manufacturers** and **retailers** run affiliate programs, the network each runs on, approximate commission, cookie window, and where to apply.

**How to read this:**
- **Program?** — `Yes` (publicly confirmed), `No` (confirmed none / ambassador-only), `Unknown` (likely exists but unverifiable from public sources).
- Anything I could not verify from a primary or strong secondary source is tagged **(unverified)** in Notes rather than guessed.
- Commission ranges reflect tiered programs (most hardware brands pay a base rate that scales with volume). Hardware commissions are low (3–12%) but average order values are high ($300–$2,500+), so absolute dollars-per-sale are strong.
- **Networks are the real gatekeepers.** Several brands (Fanatec, Logitech, Best Buy, B&H, Newegg, SkyTrak, Turtle Beach, Bushnell) live *inside* a network — you apply to the network first, then to the brand's program within it. Join the priority networks (see bottom) before chasing individual brands.

> ⚠️ **Verify on application.** Affiliate terms change often and most networks only reveal exact commission/cookie after you're approved into the specific program. Treat every number here as "approximate, confirm in-platform."

## 5. Marine Simulation (boating / helm / nav)

**Reality check:** consumer *marine simulation hardware* is a near-empty affiliate niche. There is no Fanatec/Honeycomb equivalent — marine sim runs on generic HOTAS/wheels (covered in §2–3) or professional ($$$) maritime trainers (L3Harris, VSTEP NAUTIS) that have no consumer affiliate programs. The realistic marine monetization path for IgnitionSim is **adjacent marine-electronics and boating-gear retailers** (chartplotters, fishfinders, Navionics charts), which DO have affiliate programs, plus Garmin's marine catalog under its single affiliate program.

## Recommended First Applications

Prioritized for a **new content site**: highest commission × AOV, easiest approval, best fit for sim hardware reviews/guides. Apply to these first.

1. **Amazon Associates** — universal coverage, fast approval, converts on everything. Low % but it's the baseline that earns from day one. *(Caveat: 24h cookie; must make a few sales early to stay active.)*
2. **Fanatec (Impact)** — up to 30% with bonuses, 60-day cookie, the #1 brand in sim racing. Highest commission ceiling in the whole directory. *(Confirm program is active given ownership turmoil.)*
3. **Shop Indoor Golf (CJ + Affiliately)** — 3–10% on a **~$2,300 average order**. One signup covers SkyTrak, Uneekor, FlightScope, enclosures, full bays. Best single golf-sim earner.
4. **SkyTrak (CJ)** — ~9% direct on a hot mid-range launch monitor; top manufacturer rate in golf.
5. **MOZA Racing (UpPromote)** — ~8%, fast-rising brand, easy boutique-platform approval, great content fit.
6. **Sim-Lab (Partnerize)** — up to 12%, premium high-AOV cockpits/rigs; among the best rates in sim racing.
7. **Honeycomb (Webgains)** — the cleanest flight-sim direct program; Alpha/Bravo are top-funnel MSFS buys. Free, no obligation.
8. **Logitech G (Impact)** — 4–10%, 30-day cookie, mainstream wheels + flight (Saitek/X52) + space — one program spans three disciplines with huge search volume.
9. **PlayBetter (GrowthHero)** — 5–10%, accepts adjacent-niche creators, multi-product golf/Garmin carts; pairs well with the golf section.
10. **Rain or Shine Golf** — 5–7%+, 30-day cookie, sells financeable full packages that convert well for buyers' guides.

## Networks to Join First

Joining the **network** unlocks multiple brands at once and is the real bottleneck — do these before chasing individual brands:

1. **Impact** — Fanatec, Logitech/Logitech G, Best Buy, B&H. The single most important network for IgnitionSim (covers racing + flight + space + retail).
2. **CJ Affiliate (Commission Junction)** — SkyTrak, Turtle Beach, Newegg, Shop Indoor Golf. Critical for golf + flight + PC components.
3. **Amazon Associates** — its own program; join immediately as the universal baseline.
4. **AvantLink** — Bushnell Golf, marine-electronics merchants, outdoor/marine catch-all.
5. **Partnerize** — Sim-Lab (and other premium EU sim-racing brands).
6. **FlexOffers** — Garmin (golf + marine + aviation in one), plus aggregated access to many smaller programs.
7. **Webgains** — Honeycomb (flight). Niche but the only route to Honeycomb.
8. **Boutique platforms (UpPromote, Refersion, AffiliateWP, GrowthHero)** — per-brand signups for MOZA, GT Omega, Simucube, PlayBetter respectively; quick approvals, no central gatekeeping.

> **Order of operations:** Amazon + Impact + CJ first (they cover ~70% of the catalog by SKU count), then layer in AvantLink/Partnerize/FlexOffers/Webgains, then mop up with the per-brand boutique platforms.
