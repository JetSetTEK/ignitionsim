- 3 combined Flight/Space/Marine articles per week.

- 5 articles per week minimum.
- 6 to 7 articles per week when a trend has obvious demand and buyer intent.

## Weekly Rhythm After Sprint
- Monday: trend scout plus one buyer/update article.
- Tuesday: Golf or Racing comparison.
- Wednesday: Flight, Space, or Marine article.
- Thursday: high-AOV product or ecosystem guide.
- Friday: Workbench Drop newsletter-style article with new products, deals, forum wisdom, and build inspiration.
- Optional weekend: fast trend response only when search demand is obvious.
